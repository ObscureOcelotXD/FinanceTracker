What the app does

{{ public_app.app_name }} is a read-only personal finance dashboard that aggregates balances, transaction history, cash positions, crypto visibility where available, and investment holdings so users can review net worth, cash flow, and financial trends in one place.

Plaid use case

Track and manage your finances. Current requested Plaid scope is limited to read-only transactions and investments data for personal financial management workflows.

What the app does not do

{{ public_app.app_name }} does not support payments, transfers, ACH verification, buying, selling, trading, or any other movement of funds.
