Data we collect

When you connect an account through Plaid, {{ public_app.app_name }} may receive read-only account, balance, transaction history, and investment holdings data needed to power dashboards, analytics, and holdings views.

How data is used

Data is used to aggregate financial accounts, display balances and holdings, analyze spending and cash flow, and generate decision-support insights. The app does not use Plaid for payments, transfers, ACH verification, trading, or movement of funds. Data is not sold or rented to advertisers or data brokers.

Credentials and tokens

Bank credentials are handled by Plaid Link and are not stored by {{ public_app.app_name }}. Plaid access tokens are stored server-side and are not exposed client-side.

Retention and deletion

Users may disconnect linked institutions. Linked items and locally stored financial data can be removed when no longer needed or on request.
