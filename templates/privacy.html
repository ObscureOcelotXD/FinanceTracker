Privacy Policy

{{ public_app.app_name }} uses connected financial data only to provide read-only personal finance dashboards and analytics requested by the user.

Policy location

This policy applies to the web application at {{ public_app.app_url }}, including the Plaid Management page where Plaid Link is presented.

Data we collect

When you connect an account through Plaid, {{ public_app.app_name }} may receive read-only account, balance, transaction history, and investment holdings data needed to power dashboards, analytics, and holdings views.

Your consent

Before connecting an institution via Plaid Link, you are asked to acknowledge this Privacy Policy. By checking that box and completing Plaid Link, you consent to the collection, processing, and local storage of the categories of data described here, solely to operate the features you use.

How data is used

Data is used to aggregate financial accounts, display balances and holdings, analyze spending and cash flow, and generate decision-support insights. The app does not use Plaid for payments, transfers, ACH verification, trading, or movement of funds. Data is not sold or rented to advertisers or data brokers.

Credentials and tokens

Bank credentials are handled by Plaid Link and are not stored by {{ public_app.app_name }}. Plaid access tokens are stored server-side only, never sent to the browser. Where supported by deployment configuration, those tokens may be encrypted at rest on disk.

Security (transport)

Production deployments should serve this application over HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or newer) so data between your browser and the server is encrypted in transit. Local development may use plain HTTP on localhost only.

Retention and deletion

Financial data and linked-institution metadata are kept only as long as needed to provide the product. You may disconnect linked institutions from within the app where supported. A full local data wipe may be available for operator-controlled installs. This policy is reviewed and updated when data practices change or applicable privacy expectations evolve.
