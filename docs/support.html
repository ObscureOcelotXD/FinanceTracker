FinanceTracker is a read-only personal finance dashboard for balances, transaction history, investment holdings, cash positions, and analytics.

Current use case

Track and manage your finances using read-only account aggregation and analytics.

Current scope

  • Read-only balances, transaction history, and investment holdings
  • No payments, transfers, ACH verification, trading, or movement of funds
  • Local-first application workflow while infrastructure matures
