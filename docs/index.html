FinanceTracker

FinanceTracker is a read-only personal financial management application for balances, transaction history, investment holdings, cash positions, and decision-support analytics.

The app is designed for secure account aggregation, visualization, and analytics only. It does not support payments, transfers, ACH verification, buying, selling, trading, or any movement of funds.

View support information

What it does

Aggregate accounts

Connect bank and investment accounts to review balances, transaction history, and holdings in one place.

Analyze trends

Track cash flow, portfolio visibility, and financial trends through local dashboards and analytics.

Stay read-only

FinanceTracker does not move money or initiate financial activity. It is a visibility and decision-support layer only.

Current scope

  • Read-only access to balances, transaction history, and investment holdings
  • Personal financial management and analytics
  • No onboarding flow required for broad public launch at this stage
  • Active development with local-first operation while infrastructure matures
