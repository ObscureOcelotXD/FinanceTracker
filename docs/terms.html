Terms of Use

FinanceTracker is intended for read-only personal financial management, reporting, and decision-support use.

Permitted use

The app is designed to help users review balances, transaction history, cash positions, and investment holdings in one place.

No financial advice

Analytics, summaries, and AI-generated insights are informational only and do not constitute financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.

No money movement

FinanceTracker does not support payments, transfers, ACH verification, buying, selling, trading, or any other movement of funds.

Third-party services

FinanceTracker may rely on Plaid and other third-party data providers. Availability, coverage, and data freshness may vary by institution and provider.
