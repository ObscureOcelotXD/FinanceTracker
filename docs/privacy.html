Privacy Policy

FinanceTracker uses connected financial data only to provide read-only personal finance dashboards and analytics requested by the user.

Scope

This policy applies to the deployed FinanceTracker instance where Plaid Link is offered, including the Plaid Management page (/plaid) on your app URL.

Data collected

When an account is connected through a data provider such as Plaid, FinanceTracker may receive read-only account, balance, transaction history, and investment holdings data required to power dashboards and analytics.

Your consent

Before connecting an institution via Plaid Link, the app asks you to acknowledge this policy. By doing so and completing Plaid Link, you consent to the collection, processing, and local storage of the data described here for the features you use.

How data is used

Data is used to aggregate accounts, display balances and holdings, analyze spending and cash flow, and generate decision-support insights. FinanceTracker does not use connected data for payments, transfers, ACH verification, trading, or any movement of funds.

Credentials and tokens

Bank credentials are handled by the account-linking provider and are not stored by FinanceTracker. Provider access tokens are stored server-side only, never sent to the browser. Deployments may optionally encrypt those tokens at rest on disk.

Security (transport)

Production should use HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or newer) between browser and server. Local development may use HTTP on localhost.

Data sharing

Connected financial data is not sold or rented to advertisers or data brokers.

Retention and deletion

Data is kept only as long as needed to provide the product. Users may disconnect linked institutions where the app supports it. This policy is reviewed when data practices change.
