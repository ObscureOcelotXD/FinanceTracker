Privacy Policy

FinanceTracker uses connected financial data only to provide read-only personal finance dashboards and analytics requested by the user.

Data collected

When an account is connected through a data provider such as Plaid, FinanceTracker may receive read-only account, balance, transaction history, and investment holdings data required to power dashboards and analytics.

How data is used

Data is used to aggregate accounts, display balances and holdings, analyze spending and cash flow, and generate decision-support insights. FinanceTracker does not use connected data for payments, transfers, ACH verification, trading, or any movement of funds.

Credentials and tokens

Bank credentials are handled by the account-linking provider and are not stored by FinanceTracker. Provider access tokens are stored server-side and are never exposed client-side.

Data sharing

Connected financial data is not sold or rented to advertisers or data brokers.

Retention and deletion

Linked institutions and locally stored financial data can be removed when no longer needed.
